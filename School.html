School

In grade 9, my girlfriend was elected president of the school. I was appointed Minister of Culture of the school. It was a difficult but very productive year. In the 10th grade, I was elected as president, and this was the beginning of a difficult but extremely useful path. We organized holidays and parties, helped teachers and students in everything, tried to make the life of the school brighter. There were a lot of traditions that we tried to preserve and build. This year taught me and everyone who worked with me very important things for life. Communication skills, the right communication with people, the ability to improvise and find solutions to problems - we all learned this year. I am glad that I was the president and I am glad that I had such a team.
